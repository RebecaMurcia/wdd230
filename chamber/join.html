Membership Level

Gold Membership

Price Range: $11500 - $17500 Annually

Gold Members enjoy of significant benefits that will make their business gain more visiibility. Such benefits include prominent placement in the Chamber's directory, featured listings on the Chamber's website, and special recognition at events.

Golden Members have access to exclusive resources, such as market research reports, industry insights, or educational materials. These resources can provide valuable information to help them grow and succeed in their business.

Silver Membership

Price Range: $3000 - $5800 Annually

Silver Members have access to exclusive networking events or forums, providing them with additional opportunities to connect with other businesses and community leaders.

Silver Members gain access to educational resources and workshops at discounted rates. This could include seminars, training sessions, or webinars that can benefit their business growth and development.

Bronze Membership

Price Range: $100 - $1950 Annually

Bronze Members receive foundational benefits, such as access to networking events, participation in Chamber programs, and inclusion in the Chamber's directory.

Bronze Membersreceive access to basic educational resources and workshops organized by the Chamber. These could include introductory seminars or informational sessions on relevant business topics.

NP Membership

No fee to join

Non-profit members may benefit from increased visibility through our communication channels. This could include mentions in newsletters, social media shoutouts, or features on our website, helping to raise awareness about the non-profit's mission and activities.

NP members have access to information about grants, sponsorships, or other funding opportunities available organizations. NP members receive recognition from the Chamber for their contributions to the community.
