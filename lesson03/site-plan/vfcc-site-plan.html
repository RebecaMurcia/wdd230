Vancouver Front Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Name:

Vancouver Front Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose:

Inform how the Chamber contributes to the community, promote local businesses and tourism, and inform about relevant events in the area to create engagement.

Scenarios

Where can I find a hair salon? The user is interested in learning what businesses are in the area.

How can I reach out to the Vancouver Front Chamber of Commerce? The user wants to learn where the Chamber’s contact info.

What events are happening this weekend nearby? The user would like to learn about current local events.

Color Schema

The color schema is inspired by natural colors found in beautiful nature of Washington, which is in great part what unites our community. Colors intend to represent formality, trust, and economic staility.

Typography

  • Header and subheader: Raleway bold 700 (40px -30px)
  • Text and navigation links: Raleway light 300 (16px - 10px)
